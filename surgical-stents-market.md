Key trends include the development of hybrid stents combining various materials and coatings to enhance bio-compatibility and reduce the risk of restenosis. <p><p>The surgical stents market can be categorized into three main types: metal, standard polymer, and biodegradable polymer stents. Metal stents, often made from stainless steel or cobalt-chromium alloys, provide durability and strength for long-term use. Standard polymer stents, typically made from polyester or other synthetic materials, offer flexibility and ease of deployment. Biodegradable polymer stents are designed to dissolve over time, reducing the risk of long-term complications and promoting natural healing while maintaining support during recovery.</p></p>
