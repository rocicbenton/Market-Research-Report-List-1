<p><p>The 5G PCB special ink market includes Heat Curing Ink, UV Curable Ink, and Photosensitive Ink, each serving specific functions in circuit board manufacturing. Heat Curing Ink requires elevated temperatures to cure, ensuring strong adhesion and durability. UV Curable Ink offers quick curing with ultraviolet light, enhancing production speed and efficiency. Photosensitive Ink is sensitive to light, allowing for precise patterns during the photolithography process. These inks are essential for the high-performance requirements of 5G technology in PCBs.</p></p>
