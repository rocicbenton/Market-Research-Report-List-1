<p><p>Ultraviolet (UV) curing systems encompass various technologies for drying and hardening inks, coatings, and adhesives. Mercury UV lamps are traditional sources, offering high intensity and broad spectral output, suitable for diverse applications. Metal halide lamps provide a more efficient alternative, producing UV light with lower heat and improved energy consumption. LED curing systems have gained popularity due to their energy efficiency, longevity, and environmental benefits, enabling instant curing without the warm-up time required by traditional lamps, thus enhancing manufacturing processes.</p></p>
