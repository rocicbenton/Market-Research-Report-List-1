<p><p>The oleochemical fatty acids market is divided into two main types: unsaturated fatty acids and saturated fatty acids. Unsaturated fatty acids are characterized by one or more double bonds between carbon atoms, making them generally healthier and more versatile in food and industrial applications. Saturated fatty acids, on the other hand, have no double bonds and are often solid at room temperature, commonly found in animal fats and certain plant oils. Both categories serve essential roles in food production, cosmetics, and biofuels.</p></p>
