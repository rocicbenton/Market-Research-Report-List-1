<p><p>The Art Gallery Software market is divided into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. It's often subscription-based, enabling galleries to scale costs based on usage. On-premises software, conversely, is installed locally on a gallery's hardware, offering greater control and security but requiring more upfront investment and IT resources for maintenance. Each type caters to different operational needs and preferences.</p></p>
