<p><p>The composite dental restoration material market comprises primarily resin-based composites and poly-acid modified composites. Resin-based composites are popular for their aesthetic appeal and durability, making them ideal for various dental applications like fillings and veneers. Poly-acid modified composites, often known as glass ionomer cements, offer good adhesion to tooth structure and release fluoride, enhancing their preventative benefits. Both types cater to different clinical needs, with resin composites focusing on esthetics and strength, while poly-acid modified offer bioactivity and chemical bonding.</p></p>
