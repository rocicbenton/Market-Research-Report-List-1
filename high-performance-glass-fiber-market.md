<p><p>The High Performance Glass Fiber market includes various types, each serving distinct applications. Continuous fiber consists of long strands providing excellent tensile strength and durability, ideal for aerospace and automotive sectors. Fixed length fiber, on the other hand, features shorter strands designed for enhanced thermal insulation and improved mechanical properties, suitable for construction and industrial uses. Glass wool, made from fine glass fibers, is primarily utilized for insulation purposes in buildings and HVAC systems, offering excellent thermal and acoustic performance.</p></p>
