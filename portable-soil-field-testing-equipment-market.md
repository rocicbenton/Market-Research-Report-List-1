Triaxial Tests evaluate the mechanical behavior of soil under different stress conditions. CBR Tests measure soil strength to determine pavement thickness for roads. Density & Consolidation Tests assess soil compaction and settlement characteristics. Water Permeability Tests determine the rate at which water moves through soil, essential for construction and environmental studies. Each type serves specific engineering and environmental applications.</p></p>
