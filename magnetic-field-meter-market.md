<p><p>The magnetic field meter market comprises several types, including Hall Effect meters, which measure magnetic fields through voltage variation; magnetoresistive meters (AMR, GMR, TMR), which utilize resistance changes in magnetic materials; MEMS-based meters that leverage microelectromechanical systems for compact measurements; SQUID meters, known for their extreme sensitivity in detecting minute magnetic fields; and fluxgate meters, which employ a core system to measure alternating magnetic fields. Each type serves distinct applications across industrial, scientific, and consumer sectors.</p></p>
