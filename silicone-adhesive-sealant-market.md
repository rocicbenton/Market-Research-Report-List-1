<p><ul><li>One Component Sealant</li><li>Two Component Sealant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicone adhesive sealant market is primarily categorized into one-component and two-component sealants. One-component sealants are ready to use and cure upon exposure to moisture, making them ideal for a range of applications requiring ease and convenience. In contrast, two-component sealants consist of a base and a hardening agent that must be mixed before use, offering superior strength and durability, often preferred for industrial and demanding environments. Both types serve critical roles in construction and manufacturing, ensuring effective bonding and sealing.</p></p>
