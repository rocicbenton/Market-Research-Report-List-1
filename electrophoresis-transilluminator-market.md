<p><p>The electrophoresis transilluminator market comprises various types based on the light source used. LED light transilluminators provide a safer alternative with lower risk of UV exposure and offer similar visualization capabilities. White light transilluminators are used for staining methods that require visible light, such as Coomassie blue. Other types may include specialized configurations suited for unique applications in molecular biology and biochemistry.</p></p>
