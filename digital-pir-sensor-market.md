<p><strong>The Digital PIR Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Element</li><li>Dual Element</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital PIR sensor market is categorized into three main types: single element, dual element, and others. Single element sensors utilize one pyroelectric element to detect infrared radiation, making them cost-effective for basic applications. Dual element sensors employ two elements to improve sensing capability and reduce false alarms, ideal for more complex environments. The "others" category encompasses advanced configurations, including multiple elements or integrated systems, catering to specialized applications that require enhanced sensitivity and precision in motion detection.</p></p>
