<p><ul><li>ECG Systems</li><li>Holter Monitors</li><li>Event Monitors</li><li>Implantable Loop Recorders</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiovascular monitoring and diagnostic devices market includes various types designed to assess heart health. ECG systems capture electrical activity to diagnose arrhythmias. Holter monitors provide continuous recording over 24-48 hours to detect transient abnormalities. Event monitors are portable devices that track heart activity during specific symptoms. Implantable loop recorders are inserted under the skin for long-term monitoring of irregular heartbeats. Other market segments may include stress testing systems, echocardiograms, and wearable health technology that promote cardiovascular health.</p></p>
