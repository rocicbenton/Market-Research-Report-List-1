<p><p>The Thermal Properties Analyzer market encompasses various types of devices used to evaluate thermal conductivity and heat transfer properties of materials. Heat Flow Apparatus measures steady-state heat flow, while Hot Plate Apparatus assesses thermal resistance across materials. Hot Wire Apparatus evaluates thermal conductivity in fluids or solids using a heated wire, and Flash Apparatus determines thermal diffusivity by measuring temperature response to a brief heat pulse. Additional devices, grouped under "Others," cater to specialized testing needs in diverse applications.</p></p>
