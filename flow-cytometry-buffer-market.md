<p><p>The flow cytometry buffer market is segmented into several types, including surface staining buffer, fixation buffer, viability staining buffer, and others. Surface staining buffers are used to label cell surface markers, enabling the identification of specific cell populations. Fixation buffers preserve cellular structure for analysis, while viability staining buffers differentiate live and dead cells. Other buffers may include wash buffers or those designed for specialized applications. Each type plays a critical role in enhancing the accuracy and reliability of flow cytometry results.</p></p>
