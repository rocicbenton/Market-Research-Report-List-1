<p><strong>The VRLA Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbed Glass Mat Battery</li><li>Gel Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VRLA battery market comprises two main types: Absorbed Glass Mat (AGM) and Gel batteries. AGM batteries utilize a fiberglass mat to absorb electrolyte, providing low internal resistance and optimal power for applications such as UPS systems and renewable energy storage. Gel batteries, on the other hand, use a silica-based gel to suspend electrolyte, ensuring enhanced safety and thermal stability, making them ideal for deep-cycle applications like electric vehicles and marine power. Both types offer maintenance-free operation and reliable performance.</p></p>
