<p><strong>The FRP Poles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Filament Winding</li><li>Pultrusion</li><li>Centrifugal Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>FRP (Fiber Reinforced Polymer) poles are produced through various manufacturing methods, including filament winding, pultrusion, and centrifugal casting. Filament winding involves wrapping continuous fibers around a rotating mandrel, creating strong, lightweight poles. Pultrusion involves pulling continuous fibers through a resin bath and then into a heated die to form composite shapes, offering efficiency and uniformity. Centrifugal casting uses rotational force to distribute resin and fibers within a mold, producing highly durable poles suitable for various applications, from utility to decorative uses.</p></p>
