<p><p>A shot peening machine is an industrial device used to improve the mechanical properties of materials, particularly metals, through a process that bombards the surface of the material with small spherical shots. This process induces compressive stress, enhancing fatigue strength and resistance to stress corrosion cracking. Shot peening is commonly employed in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing, where durability and performance are critical.</p><p>The Shot Peening Machine Market is experiencing steady growth due to increasing demand for lightweight and durable components in the aerospace and automotive sectors.
