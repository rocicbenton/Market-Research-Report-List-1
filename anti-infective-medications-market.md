<p><p>The anti-infective medications market is categorized into three main types: antivirals, antibacterials, and antifungals. Antivirals are designed to treat viral infections by inhibiting virus replication, crucial for diseases like HIV and influenza. Antibacterials target bacterial infections, either killing bacteria or inhibiting their growth, essential for conditions like pneumonia and strep throat. Antifungals combat fungal infections, useful in treating conditions like candidiasis and athlete's foot. Together, these segments play a vital role in managing infectious diseases globally.</p></p>
