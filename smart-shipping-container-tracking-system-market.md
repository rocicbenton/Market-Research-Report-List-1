<p><p>The Smart Shipping Container Tracking System market consists of two primary types: Maritime Tracking Systems and Land Tracking Systems. On the other hand, Land Tracking Systems manage container movements on land, incorporating RFID, IoT sensors, and GPS to optimize logistics, reduce delays, and enhance supply chain visibility throughout the inland transport phase.</p></p>
