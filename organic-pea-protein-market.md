<p><p>The organic pea protein market is segmented into three main types: isolates, concentrates, and textured. Isolates have the highest protein content, often exceeding 80%, and are primarily used in protein powders and supplements. Concentrates contain around 70% protein and retain more of the pea's nutrients, making them ideal for smoothies and food products. Textured pea protein is produced through processing, creating a meat-like texture suitable for plant-based meat alternatives and enhancing the texture of various dishes.</p></p>
