<p><strong>The Polypropylene Packaging Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast Polypropylene (CPP) Film</li><li>Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polypropylene packaging films market includes two main types: Cast Polypropylene (CPP) Film and Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) Film. CPP film is characterized by its excellent clarity, heat sealability, and flexibility, making it suitable for flexible packaging applications. BOPP film, on the other hand, is produced through a biaxial orientation process, offering superior strength, moisture resistance, and barrier properties. These features make BOPP ideal for food packaging, labels, and other applications requiring durability and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
