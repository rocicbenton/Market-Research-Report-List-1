<p><p>Elastomeric rubber foam insulation materials are categorized into several types based on their composition. NBR (Nitrile Butadiene Rubber) based insulation offers excellent thermal resistance and is oil-resistant, making it suitable for HVAC applications. EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) based insulation is known for its weather resistance and durability, ideal for outdoor use. Chloroprene based insulation provides good flexibility and fire resistance. Other types may include silicone and neoprene, catering to specialized applications in various industries.</p></p>
