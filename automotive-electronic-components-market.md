<p><strong>The Automotive Electronic Components Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Components</li><li>Passive Components</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive electronic components market is divided into active and passive components. Active components, such as microcontrollers, sensors, and amplifiers, require an external power source to operate and are crucial for functions like control systems and safety features. Passive components, including resistors, capacitors, and inductors, do not need power to function and are used for filtering, energy storage, and signal conditioning. Together, these components enhance vehicle performance, safety, and comfort, driving advancements in automotive technology.</p></p>
