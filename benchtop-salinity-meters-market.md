<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Dual Channel</li><li>Multi Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Benchtop salinity meters are classified into three main types based on the number of channels they offer. Single-channel meters are designed for measuring salinity in one sample at a time, ideal for simple applications. Dual-channel meters allow simultaneous measurement of two samples, enhancing efficiency in comparative analysis. Multi-channel meters, capable of measuring multiple samples concurrently, are suited for laboratories that require high throughput and detailed analysis. Each type serves different needs, balancing complexity and convenience in salinity measurement.</p></p>
