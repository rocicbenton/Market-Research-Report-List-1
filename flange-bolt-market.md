<p><p>The flange bolt market primarily consists of two types: partial thread flange bolts and full thread flange bolts. Partial thread flange bolts feature a limited threaded section, providing a secure connection while allowing for flexibility in alignment and installation. In contrast, full thread flange bolts have a fully threaded design, offering greater grip and load distribution. These bolts are utilized in various applications, including machinery and construction, where strength and reliability are essential for maintaining structural integrity.</p></p>
