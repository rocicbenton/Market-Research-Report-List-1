<p><ul><li>Horizontal Axis</li><li>Vertical Axis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Quiet Wind Turbine market comprises two primary types: Horizontal Axis and Vertical Axis turbines. Horizontal Axis turbines have blades that rotate around a horizontal shaft, typically mounted on tall towers, and are known for their efficiency and higher energy output. In contrast, Vertical Axis turbines feature blades that rotate around a vertical shaft, allowing them to capture wind from any direction and operate in turbulent conditions, making them ideal for urban environments. Both types aim to minimize noise while generating sustainable energy.</p></p>
