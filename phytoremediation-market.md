<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Phytoremediation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phytosequestration</li><li>Rhizodegradation</li><li>Phytohydraulics</li><li>Phytoextraction</li><li>Phytovolatilization</li><li>Phytodegradation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The phytoremediation market encompasses various techniques for environmental cleanup using plants. Phytosequestration focuses on absorbing CO2 and heavy metals in biomass. Rhizodegradation utilizes plant roots to enhance microbial degradation of contaminants. Phytohydraulics involves plants to control groundwater movement and mitigate contamination spread. Phytoextraction is the process of plants absorbing and concentrating pollutants from soil. Phytovolatilization enables plants to uptake and release volatile contaminants into the atmosphere. Phytodegradation involves plants breaking down pollutants into less harmful substances.</p></p>
