<p><p>Percutaneous Transluminal Coronary Angioplasty Drug Eluting Balloon (DEB) Catheters are specialized medical devices used to treat narrowed or blocked coronary arteries. These catheters deliver medication directly to the arterial wall during balloon angioplasty, minimizing restenosis (re-narrowing of the artery) and improving patient outcomes. The precision of DEB technology allows for effective drug delivery while preserving arterial integrity, making them an essential tool in interventional cardiology.</p><p>The market for DEB catheters is poised for significant growth, driven by increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, advancements in medical technology, and a rising aging population. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.2% during the forecast period. Key trends include the development of next-generation drug-eluting balloons with enhanced drug formulations and improved balloon designs that increase the procedural efficacy. Furthermore, the integration of real-time imaging and monitoring during procedures is enhancing safety and effectiveness. As healthcare systems shift towards minimally invasive surgeries to reduce recovery time and complications, the adoption of DEB catheters is anticipated to rise, further bolstering market expansion. Overall, these innovations and demographic factors are shaping the future of the DEB catheter market.</p></p>
