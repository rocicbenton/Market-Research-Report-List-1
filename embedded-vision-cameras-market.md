<p><strong>The Embedded Vision Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Embedded Vision Cameras market encompasses two primary types: area scan and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture an entire frame at once, making them ideal for applications requiring detailed image analysis in stationary scenes, such as quality inspection and object recognition. In contrast, line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, excelling in high-speed applications like conveyor belt inspection and web monitoring where continuous material movement occurs. Both types play crucial roles in automation, robotics, and industrial processes.</p></p>
