<p><p>Biologics in spine surgery encompass various materials and therapies that promote healing and support spinal fusion. Bone grafts are harvested from the patient or a donor, aiding in bone regeneration. Bone graft substitutes, like synthetic materials, mimic the properties of bone and facilitate healing without harvesting. Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP) involves concentrating platelets from the patient’s blood to enhance tissue repair and reduce inflammation. Other market types may include stem cell therapies or demineralized bone matrix products, expanding treatment options.</p></p>
